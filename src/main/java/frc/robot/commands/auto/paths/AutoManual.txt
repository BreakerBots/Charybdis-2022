Nameing Convention:
    - What the path does_Starting position
    - EX: Pickup1_Shoot2_H1 (the path intakes one ball, shoots two balls, and starts at position 1 on the hangars side of the field)

Paths:
    paths are sequences of actions that the robot will preform during autonomous.
        - HOW TO USE: Put each of the desired actions in a sequential command group, and call them in the order in which they must be preformed.
